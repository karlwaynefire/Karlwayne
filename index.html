Fire Safety Tips

  • Install Smoke Detectors: Make sure every floor and room has a working smoke detector. Test monthly and replace batteries twice a year.
  • Keep Exits Clear: Never block doors or windows with furniture or clutter—clear exits can save lives in emergencies.
  • Don’t Overload Outlets: Plug one high-wattage appliance into each outlet to prevent overheating and electrical fires.
  • Stay in the Kitchen: Never leave cooking unattended. Unattended stoves are a leading cause of house fires.
  • Have an Escape Plan: Create a family evacuation plan with two exit routes and practice it regularly.
  • Use Heaters Safely: Keep flammable items at least 3 feet away from space heaters. Turn them off when you leave the room or go to bed.
